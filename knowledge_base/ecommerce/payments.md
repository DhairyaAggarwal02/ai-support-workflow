# Payments Policy

## Accepted Payment Methods
The store accepts major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal.

## Failed Payments
If payment fails, the customer should verify their billing details, card expiry date, and available funds.

## Duplicate Charges
Duplicate charges should be escalated to billing support for review.

## Currency
Prices are displayed in the customer's selected currency when supported.

## Payment Security
The store does not store full credit card numbers directly.
