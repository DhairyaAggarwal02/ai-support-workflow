# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping
Standard shipping usually takes 5 to 7 business days after the order has been processed.

## Express Shipping
Express shipping usually takes 2 to 3 business days after the order has been processed.

## Order Processing
Orders are usually processed within 1 to 2 business days.

## Lost Packages
If a package has not arrived after 10 business days, the customer should contact support with their order number.

## International Shipping
International delivery times vary by country. Customers may be responsible for customs duties, taxes, or import fees.
