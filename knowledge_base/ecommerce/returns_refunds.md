# Returns and Refunds Policy

## Return Window
Customers may request a return within 30 days of delivery.

## Refund Processing
Refunds are usually processed within 5 to 10 business days after the returned item is received.

## Opened Items
Opened or used items may not be eligible for a refund unless the product arrived damaged or defective.

## Damaged Items
If an item arrives damaged, the customer should contact support within 7 days and provide photos of the damage.

## Refund Escalation
Refund disputes, chargebacks, or repeated refund requests should be escalated to a human support agent.
