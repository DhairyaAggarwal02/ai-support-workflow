# Subscription Policy

## Managing Subscriptions
Customers can pause, cancel, or modify their subscription from their account dashboard.

## Cancellation
Subscription cancellations should be completed before the next billing date to avoid renewal charges.

## Renewal Reminders
Customers may receive a reminder before their subscription renews.

## Failed Renewal
If a renewal payment fails, the system may retry the payment or notify the customer to update their billing information.

## Subscription Escalation
Customers requesting exceptions, refunds for renewal charges, or complaints about cancellation should be escalated.
