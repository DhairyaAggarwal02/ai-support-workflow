# Order Tracking Policy

## Tracking Information
Customers receive tracking information by email after the order has shipped.

## Tracking Not Updated
Tracking information may take 24 to 48 hours to update after the shipping carrier receives the package.

## Missing Tracking Email
If a customer did not receive a tracking email, they should check their spam folder or contact support with their order number.

## Package Not Moving
If tracking has not updated for several business days, the customer should contact support for assistance.
