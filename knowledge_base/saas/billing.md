# SaaS Billing Policy

## Accepted Payment Methods
The platform accepts major credit cards and debit cards.

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails, users should update their billing method from the billing dashboard.

## Duplicate Charges
Duplicate charges should be escalated to billing support.

## Invoice Requests
Users can download invoices from the billing section of their account.

## Tax Information
Tax information may vary by region and should be reviewed in the billing dashboard.
