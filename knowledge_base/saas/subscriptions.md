# SaaS Subscription Policy

## Plan Changes
Users can upgrade or downgrade their plan from the account billing dashboard.

## Cancellation
Users can cancel their subscription from the billing dashboard before the next renewal date.

## Renewal
Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled before the renewal date.

## Refunds
Refund requests for subscription charges may require human review depending on the account history.

## Trial Period
Trial access may expire automatically unless the user upgrades to a paid plan.
